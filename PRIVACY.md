# Privacy Policy for Page2Data

Last updated: 2025-04-25

**Page2Data does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The extension only:
- Reads the content of the current webpage when you click the extension icon, solely to detect tables and structured data for preview
- Stores your Pro license activation status locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local)
- Downloads CSV/JSON files to your computer when you explicitly click export

No data is sent to any server. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies.
